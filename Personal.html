Personal

Ideology = Passion + Creativeness + Vision
(For science)

• Passion--(the key to the palace of science)

The key to motivate myself is the passion for the computer vision. Looking around the famous scientists around the world, everyone loves what they did, thus they can stick to the direction they followed and achieve the amazing things. I admire the person much who can insist on their direction. Take the great Prof. Hinton for example, his perseverance to the neural networks attract me so much and finally, through a nearly 20 years hard working bring the machine learning out of the stalemate and make the deep learning prevailed everywhere.

I want to be a person like him, dedicating myself into the research and follow the direction I loved, computer vision. Thus, from the beginning of 3rd year, I participated in the research lab and conducted several projects in computer vision area. In the future I want to make the machine vision more intelligent and like the human visual mechanism to handle the complex scenery.

• Creative Mind--(enable me unlimited boundary to elegantly dance in the palace)

Every time, I would use the example of Newton to illustrated this point. As a matter of fact, why Newton is so famous is not because he is the best at solving complex equations in his era, it’s just because he would think about why the apple would fall down rather than up. Imagine that, if you give a task to let someone to solve the gravity equation, there would must be hundreds of persons can handle it in that era. Thus, beyond the kinds of ability, the most key point in the science area, in my view, is a creative mind and open thoughts. The ability to question and thinking.

Thus I pay much attention to build myself a habit to think and try to make innovation through well observation. Beyond the ability, I want to maintain myself active and creative especially in mind and thoughts in the future.

• Vision--(decide attitude)

Vision or outlook always decides the way you view the problem or question whether in life or academics. In another words, it decides the attitude. And this is also one main reason why I want to go abroad to have further education and experienced more.
